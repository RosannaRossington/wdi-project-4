Originally developed by Nike The Materials Sustainability Index (MSI) is a "cradle-to-gate" measurement of a material's environmental impact.

The assessment returns a total numeric score on a 50-point scale, with a higher score representing better performance in the selected environmental impact areas.

The cradle-to-gate life cycle considers the journey of raw materials to a finished textile, ready to be shipped to a company.

All materials are scored using the same environmental impact areas and scale to enable relative comparisons.
